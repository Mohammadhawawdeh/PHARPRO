A comprehensive, regulation-aligned curriculum covering the full CSV lifecycle from planning through closure — designed and delivered by practising PHARPRO consultants.

  • EU GMP Annex 11 requirements

Validation Lifecycle Documentation

Audit Trail & Data Integrity

  • ALCOA+ data integrity principles
  • Common data integrity failures

Change Control & Periodic Review

  • Change control for validated systems
  • Vendor-initiated change assessment
  • Periodic review requirements
  • System retirement and decommissioning
