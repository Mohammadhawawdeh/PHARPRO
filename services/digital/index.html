Regulatory Alignment

Every digital system must meet the same regulatory bar

Pharma regulators have issued clear guidance that digital and AI tools in GxP environments are subject to the same validation and data integrity requirements as any other computerised system.

Framework Scope Applies To
FDA 21 CFR Part 11 Electronic records, e-signatures, and audit trails for FDA-regulated systems All US FDA-regulated digital systems
EU GMP Annex 11 Validation, data integrity, and change control for computerised systems in GMP operations EMA / EU-regulated computerised systems
