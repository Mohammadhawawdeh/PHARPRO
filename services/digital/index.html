Regulatory Alignment

Every digital system must meet the same regulatory bar

Pharma regulators have issued clear guidance that digital and AI tools in GxP environments are subject to the same validation and data integrity requirements as any other computerised system.

Framework Scope Applies To
FDA 21 CFR Part 11 Electronic records, e-signatures, and audit trails for FDA-regulated systems All US FDA-regulated digital systems
EU GMP Annex 11 Validation, data integrity, and change control for computerised systems in GMP operations EMA / EU-regulated computerised systems
FDA AI/ML Guidance Action plan and pre-submission programme for AI/ML-based software in regulated contexts AI/ML tools used in drug development & manufacturing
ISPE GAMP 5 (2nd Ed.) Risk-based approach to validation of computerised systems including cloud & SaaS All regulated pharma digital environments

Common questions on pharma digitalisation

Pharma 4.0 applies Industry 4.0 principles — IoT, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and advanced data analytics — to pharmaceutical manufacturing and quality systems. It enables real-time process monitoring, predictive quality, and paperless GMP operations while maintaining full regulatory compliance.
AI tools used in GxP environments must be validated under the same principles as any other computerised system — defined intended use, risk classification, IQ/OQ/PQ where applicable, and ongoing monitoring.
