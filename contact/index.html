Common questions

How quickly can PHARPRO start work?

For urgent situations — imminent inspection, Warning Letter response, or critical gap — PHARPRO can mobilise within 3–5 business days. Standard project kickoff is typically 1–2 weeks after proposal agreement.

Which countries does PHARPRO serve?

PHARPRO is based in Amman, Jordan, and serves pharmaceutical clients across the MENA region — including UAE, Saudi Arabia, Egypt, Kuwait, and Bahrain — as well as clients in Europe and beyond. Remote project delivery is available for international clients.

Is the first consultation really free?

Yes. The discovery call is free, confidential, and carries no obligation. We'll listen to your situation, identify the most pressing compliance gaps, and explain how we can help — without a sales pitch. You decide whether to proceed.

Do you sign NDAs before discussing projects?

Yes — PHARPRO is happy to execute a mutual NDA before discussing any project details. Client confidentiality is standard practice for us. Contact us and we will send the NDA template for your review.

Can PHARPRO work on-site or only remotely?

Both. For clients in Jordan and the wider MENA region, on-site engagement is our default. For international clients, we offer structured remote delivery with periodic on-site visits for key project milestones. Many gap assessments and CSV projects are successfully delivered in a hybrid model.

What is a free compliance assessment?

The free assessment is a structured 30–45 minute call where our consultant reviews your current compliance programme, identifies your highest-risk gaps, and recommends a practical prioritised action plan. You leave the call with clarity on what needs to happen first — regardless of whether you engage PHARPRO for further work.
