What We Deliver

Full CQV lifecycle — from DQ to requalification

Every qualification activity generates structured, traceable documentation built to withstand regulatory scrutiny at any stage of the equipment lifecycle.

Design Qualification (DQ)

Verification that proposed equipment design meets GMP and operational requirements — reviewed against URS and engineering specifications before procurement.

Installation Qualification (IQ)

Documented verification that equipment is installed correctly per manufacturer specifications, utility connections are confirmed, and safety systems are in place.

Operational Qualification (OQ)

Testing that equipment operates within specified limits across the full operational range — including worst-case and boundary conditions.

Performance Qualification (PQ)

Demonstration that equipment consistently performs under actual production conditions — using production-representative loads and materials.

Thermal Mapping Studies

Temperature and humidity distribution mapping for cold rooms, warehouses, refrigerators, incubators, and autoclaves — aligned with WHO GDP and EU GDP requirements.

Requalification & Periodic Review

Planned requalification programmes, change control assessment for equipment modifications, and periodic qualification review to maintain validated state.

FAQ

Common CQV questions

CQV stands for Commissioning, Qualification, and Validation. It is the structured process of verifying that pharmaceutical equipment and utilities are installed correctly (IQ), operate as intended (OQ), and consistently deliver the required performance (PQ) under actual production conditions.
PHARPRO qualifies a broad range of equipment including autoclaves, HVAC systems, freeze dryers, cold rooms and warehouses, water systems (PW, WFI), cleanrooms, manufacturing equipment, and laboratory instruments.
Thermal mapping is the measurement and documentation of temperature distribution throughout a storage area, refrigerator, freezer, cold room, or warehouse. It is required under WHO Good Storage and Distribution Practices, EU GDP, and most national regulatory frameworks for pharmaceutical storage.
